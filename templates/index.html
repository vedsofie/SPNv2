Features

Featured probes

Florbetaben

Diagnostic PET probe to image ß-amyloid neuritic plaque density in the brains of adult patients with cognitive impairment to evaluate Alzheimer’s disease and other cognitive impairments.

FDHT

Dihydrotrotestosterone analog PET probe with high affinity for androgen receptor (AR) used to image AR expression in castration-resistant prostate cancer and measure pharmacodynamics of AR-binding drugs.

MK-6240

PET probe designed to bind neurofibrillary tau tangles in Alzheimer’s disease.
